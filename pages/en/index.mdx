Built as a modular blockchain, NERO Chain stresses providing value to applications over infrastructure-level storage. NERO Chain changes traditional blockchain economics by letting dApps gather and share transaction value, therefore ensuring that the success of an application benefits its developers and users instead of just the underlying network. With its complete EVM compatibility and high-performance settling layer, Nero offers amazing scalability and flexibility, including native account abstraction, paymaster-driven gas sponsorship, and current technologies like Blockspace 2.0 which provide developers with a flawless experience.
