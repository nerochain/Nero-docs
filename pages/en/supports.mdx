# Support & Resources

Get help and connect with the NERO Chain community through these official channels:

## Community Support

- **[Discord](https://discord.gg/nerochainofficial)**: Join our active community for real-time technical support, development discussions, and network updates.
  
- **[Telegram](https://t.me/nerochainofficial)**: Connect with our team and other NERO Chain users.

- **[Twitter](https://twitter.com/Nerochain_io)**: Follow us for announcements, news and updates.

## Developer Resources

- **[GitHub Repository](https://github.com/nerochain)**: Access our open-source code, report issues, or contribute.
  
- **[Documentation](https://docs.nerochain.org)**: Comprehensive guides, tutorials and API references.
